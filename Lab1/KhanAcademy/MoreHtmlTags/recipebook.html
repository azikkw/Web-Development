Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Serves: Knife, frying pan, cutting board
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken thigh 300 grams
Cucumbers are fresh 150 grams
Fresh tomatoes 150 grams
Carrot 70 grams
Lavash 2 pieces

How to cook shawarma with chicken:

Step 1: Cut the chicken into thin layers and lightly beat the meat with a wooden hammer.

Step 2: Spread a layer of chicken on a plate and sprinkle with half of the spices: dry garlic, cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, salt and sprinkle with 1 tbsp.a spoonful of vegetable oil. Then spread the second layer of chicken and sprinkle with spices and sprinkle with vegetable oil.

Step 3: Leave the chicken to marinate for 30 minutes.

Step 4:Cut cabbage, cucumbers and onions into thin strips, grate carrots on a coarse grater, cut tomatoes into half rings.

Step 5:Mix cabbage and carrots in a bowl, add 1/2 tsp. salt, mix and knead with your hand.

Step 6: Preheat the grill pan and fry the marinated chicken layers on medium heat on each side for 3 minutes until cooked.

Step 7: Cut the finished chicken into strips.

Step 8: If the pita bread is large, cut it into 2 parts. We spread the filling on the pita bread, retreating from the edges of the pita bread, the bottom indent should be larger. Spread 2 tablespoons of garlic sauce and distribute the filling in the shape of a rectangle.

Step 9: Put a layer of cabbage with carrots on top 1-2 tbsp.l., pour 1-2 tsp. of garlic sauce.

Step 10: Add a layer of chicken on top, then cucumbers, tomatoes and onions, and again, 1-2 tsp garlic sauce.

Step 11: Tightly roll up the tube from the side of the filling, make two turns, bend the edges and turn it to the end of the pita bread.

Step 12: In a preheated frying pan, fry the pita bread for 1 minute on each side. The shawarma is ready.
